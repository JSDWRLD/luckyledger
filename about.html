About Us

LuckyLedger is a dedicated Dapp that focuses on ensuring fair results and hosting of raffle-style lotteries. The team created this to provide a platform where people can host and circulate money among a community. This is accomplished by removing the need to pay taxes on big rewards, avoiding malicious actors, and creating trust through smart contracts.

The Dapp allows users to host their own raffles: setting the ticket fee, duration of the raffle, and the ticket limit. As more users buy tickets, the pot grows until a winner is chosen when the timer reaches zero. When a winner is drawn, the host gets 20% of the pot, and the rest goes to the winner, facilitated by our smart contract.
